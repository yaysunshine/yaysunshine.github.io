So how'd you get into coding, Catie?

Short version: Found out about this little peach called coding. My love (and understanding) of it has been a blossoming flower.

Long(er) version: My dad is a general contractor. He was working on an office over summer 2014 with a bunch of people on their computers like the facebook movie. He inquired what was going on, and his client recommended DevBootcamp. I did the required links to get into the bootcamp and started falling in love with coding. It has been a process. Lots of progress.

Where do you want to go with this career?

Everywhere. I am a woman, I am young, I want to change the ratio and take my career far. I want to be successful. I am hard working. I love coding because I love to collaborate; I love to tinker; I love to learn. There is so much to learn. I will never want to stop.

I am so excited.
