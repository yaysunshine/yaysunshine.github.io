The Diversity In This Industry Is Pathetic

13 Dec 2014

Problems in the tech world...ok. I need to write about the really, truly drastic need for women in the tech industry. My fellow DBC Fiddler Crab Rebecca Holzschuh has a really good blog on it. I, too, am going to get passionate about this.

We get hired less and paid less (if we are even hired in the first place). STOP UNDERVALUING YOURSELF. NOW.

In August 2014, Google recorded that 83% of its work force is male. What the hell, people. Do I need to give anymore reason why this is wrong? Women make up half of the fucking population. And all we can do is NOT hire them?

This is bad. But I can give a possible reason.

People of any business hire who they know.

So.

White men will hire other white men.

Because that's who they know.

I am just starting in this industry, but I am over it. I want to change this. Like the beautiful, inspiring women of write/speak/code.

There are solutions.

Get known. Put yourself out there. HELP other women!! Don't be catty! Give chances, take risks, DO IT.

FEMALES:

GO OUT THERE AND KICK ASS

I dare you.<3

~ Catie Kennedy ~
